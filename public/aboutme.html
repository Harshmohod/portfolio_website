About Me

Hi, I’m Harsh Prakash Mohod, a passionate data analyst with a strong business mindset and a keen eye for unlocking insights that drive smarter decisions. With a solid foundation in data analysis, I specialize in transforming complex data into actionable strategies. I’m also a trader, navigating the world of markets with precision and strategic foresight. My goal is always to leverage my skills to create value, innovate, and stay ahead of the curve in both tech and business. Beyond my professional endeavors, I’m a person who thrives on living life according to my own values. I believe in the importance of integrity, hard work, and loyalty. My family is my core, and I draw much of my strength and inspiration from the people I care about. I’m also deeply passionate about sports, especially basketball, which has taught me resilience, discipline, and the importance of collaboration. As College Sports President, I’ve had the privilege of leading teams and organizing events that foster community and excellence. When I’m not coding or competing, you’ll likely find me enjoying a bike ride – it’s my way to disconnect, recharge, and keep my mind clear. In everything I do, whether in tech or sports, I strive for growth and challenge myself to push the limits of what’s possible. I look forward to leveraging my skills, passion, and leadership in my next professional opportunity.
